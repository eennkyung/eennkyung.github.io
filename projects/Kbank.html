K Bank
K Bank is an internet bank created in Korea for the first time in 25 years.
While existing banks have limited hours of operation and complex procedures, K Bank is able to do all banking services at the ease of your mobile without having to go to branches and bankers.
This project improved usability and redesigned as K Bank was created fast.
I focus on designing a clean complexity with simple and design clarity.
