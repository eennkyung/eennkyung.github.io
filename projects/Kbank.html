K bank
K bank is an internet bank created for the first time in 25 years of Korea. While existing banks have limited hours and complex procedures, K bank is to be able to do all banking services on the mobile without branches and bankers.
This project improved usability and redesigned the missed as K-bank was created fast. I designed focus on clean up complexity with simple and clairty design.
